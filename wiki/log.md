Click-driven inline mini-form `InlineFeaturePopover` adopts the same deferred-unmount machinery the 2026-05-30 host-canopy-union tooltip ship landed: a `displayed: InlineFormPayload | null` mirror state lives one exit-transition longer than `useInlineFormStore.active`, `exiting: boolean` flips at the same time `active` goes null, `visible: boolean` flips via `useLayoutEffect` after mount so the enter transition fires from `opacity: 0`/`translateY(2px)` тЖТ `opacity: 1`/`translateY(0)`.
