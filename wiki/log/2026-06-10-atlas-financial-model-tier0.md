## Amanah

**CLEAN** (screened at Phase 3f kickoff; verdict re-affirmed on transcription). Every recorded mechanism is ordinary co-owner cost-sharing among community members -- NOT riba, NOT gharar, NOT `bayʿ mā laysa ʿindak`, and NOT advance-purchase / CSRA / salam framing ([[fiqh-csra-erased-2026-05-04]]):

- **Buy-in** = equity in the commons (a share of land + Phase 1 infrastructure + a community-fund deposit), i.e. co-ownership capital, NOT advance-purchase of future yield.
- **Levy** = cost-sharing for shared operations (maintenance, insurance, reserve contribution, operations fund).
- **Fund governance** = custody + authorisation + reporting; no interest instrument.
- **Hardship Tier-1 deferral** is interest-free ("deferred without penalty", repaid over 6 months with NO markup) -- a qard-hasan-like forbearance, the cleanest possible treatment.
- **Reserves** = pooled communal savings against major infrastructure renewal.
- **Ratification** = a sign-off gate.

No capital-channel or sale-channel construct requiring the "capital partners & allies" label is present, so no `scopeNotes` flag is required. Content encoded verbatim per the standing directive (never reword/omit). One authored-prose nuance: the mockup's levy feeds-note references a cross-mockup "community loan tension resolved in infrastructure strategy"; the per-mode `FeedsNote` copy is authored UI guidance (not verbatim catalogue content), so it points to this capture's own downstream items and the transitional-reduction-is-not-a-loan framing rather than reproducing an unscreened cross-mockup loan reference -- consistent with how prior captures author their feeds notes. **Downstream operating note (not a recorded figure here):** if a community credit-union/bank deposit accrues interest, that interest must be purified/donated.
