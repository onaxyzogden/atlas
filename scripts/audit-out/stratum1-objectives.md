## Universal (every project)

### U-S1.1 — A clear vision, goals & stewardship capacity

- **Objective id:** `s1-vision`
- **Short title:** Vision, goals & stewardship capacity
- **Focused question:** What is this project for, what does success look like, and what resources does the steward have to work with?
- **Completion gate:** A bounded, evidence-grounded vision is approved with clear success criteria and capacity constraints documented.
- **Act handoff:** Vision & Capacity Brief

**Tasks**

- **Purpose & intent**
  - Define the primary purpose and land use type for this project
  - Define 3-5 measurable success criteria for the first planning cycle
  - Confirm the primary steward and any co-stewards for this project _(optional)_
  - Classify vision elements as committed vs. aspirational
- **Capacity & constraints**
  - Inventory available labour - hours per week, seasonal variation, skill level
  - Inventory available capital - initial budget and estimated annual operating budget
  - Identify non-negotiables and hard constraints
  - Record assumptions and known unknowns

### U-S1.2 — Establish site boundaries & legal constraints

- **Objective id:** `s1-boundaries`
- **Short title:** Site boundaries & legal constraints
- **Focused question:** What are the legal, physical, and regulatory boundaries within which this project must operate?
- **Completion gate:** All legal constraints and boundary conditions are mapped, recorded, and reviewed. No design work proceeds into areas of legal ambiguity.
- **Act handoff:** Legal & Boundary Constraints Brief

**Tasks**

- **Title & boundary**
  - Map property boundaries on base layer
  - Obtain and verify current title and deed documents
- **Legal & permit obligations**
  - Identify all easements, rights of way, and encumbrances
  - Check zoning and permitted land uses
  - Identify water rights and entitlements
  - Record covenant, heritage, or conservation obligations
  - Note required permits for planned activities - building, earthworks, water harvesting

### U-S1.3 — A mapped picture of stakeholders & community

- **Objective id:** `s1-stakeholders`
- **Short title:** Stakeholders & community
- **Focused question:** Who has an interest in, connection to, or jurisdiction over this land and project?
- **Completion gate:** Stakeholder map is complete. No known relationships unrecorded.
- **Act handoff:** Stakeholder Register

**Tasks**

- **Neighbours & authority**
  - Map immediate neighbours and shared boundary relationships
  - Identify local authority contacts and relevant officers
  - Record any Indigenous land relationships or cultural obligations
- **Networks & partnerships**
  - Identify community members affected by or interested in the project
  - Note existing conflict, goodwill, or partnership relationships
  - Record preferred communication channels for each stakeholder group
