## Adding new per-kind models

1. Drop the GLB at `public/models/structures/<kind>.glb` (or add the
   kind to `apps/web/scripts/gen-structure-glbs.mjs` if you want
   procedural authoring).
2. Append a row above with author, source, licence, notes.
3. Update the kind's entry in
   `apps/web/src/v3/plan/canvas/elementHeights.ts` so its `glbUrl`
   points at the new file.
