## Instrumented for the long horizon

Three Streams Farm is sampled on the MDPI Apricot-Lane cadence — Year 0, Year 5, Year 9 — the rhythm that lets a stewardship arc read as a curve rather than a snapshot. The A-series surfaces are the instrument: a regeneration monitor that carries soil organic matter on the cropped half from the 1.4–1.9 % baseline through the 2.6–3.1 % pasture band toward the Y5 polyculture range, a biodiversity monitor that follows bird-species richness from the eroded baseline toward the roughly 2.4× Y5 reading, and an infiltration trajectory that records what the keyline swales gave back to the watershed.

This is not a dashboard for the next quarter. It is an instrument for the next generation, recording what the land does over years.
