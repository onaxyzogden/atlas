## The land tells you what it needs next

By Year 5, the polyculture on Three Streams Farm is visibly mature — silvopasture canopy unioned across overlapping guilds, the three streams shaded through roughly seventy per cent of their on-farm reach, organic matter in the 3.5–4.2 % band across former row-crop ground. From Y5 onward the work is no longer establishment. It is judgement.

The A-series readings feed the operating dashboards; the dashboards discipline the next year's decisions. Where succession is racing ahead of the design, the recommendation is restraint. In a drought year, the fallback mix shifts toward deeper-rooted species. When pollinator diversity plateaus, the guild composition is adjusted, not the scale.

The land tells you what it needs next. The stewardship loop is built to listen.
