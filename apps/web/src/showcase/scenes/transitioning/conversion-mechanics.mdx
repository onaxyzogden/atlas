## From continuous corn to working polyculture

If you are operating a cash-crop rotation today, the Y0 → Y2 mechanics on Three Streams Farm are a sequence you can read as a working operator. The land begins as a fourteen-year unbroken corn-soy signature. In Year 1, the bare winter fallow is replaced — winter rye and crimson clover after the last corn harvest, tillage radish and oats over-seeded into standing corn — while the first keyline swales are cut along the contours of the two steeper sub-watersheds and hedgerow whips begin in the nursery.

By Year 2, the riparian buffer along all three tributaries is established, the first orchard block is planted on the south-facing bench, and the kitchen-garden ground is moving. None of it skips a step.
