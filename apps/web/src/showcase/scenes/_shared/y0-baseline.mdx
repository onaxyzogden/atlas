## Year 0 — The starting line

Purchased in 2024. The east and west fields had been in continuous corn for nineteen years. Soil organic matter in the cropped half measured **1.65%** — the worm count was **0.8 per square metre**, and no anecic (deep-burrowing) earthworms were found. Infiltration on the cropped soil clocked at **11.8 mm/hr** — half the rate of the still-living pasture half.

The MDPI Apricot Lane Y0/5/9 protocol was used end-to-end to anchor the baseline. Eleven bird species were observed across six 8-minute point counts.

This is the ground every later number reads against.
