## Year 5 — Mid-arc maturity (projected)

By Year 5 the perennial systems should be doing real work. Silvopasture savanna in the central west, the orchard polyculture maturing in the kitchen-garden expansion, the hedgerow grown enough to throw windbreak shadow well into the east field. Cropped soil organic matter is projected to reach **~3.6%**.

These numbers are forward-projected from canon milestones and the MDPI Apricot Lane Y0/5/9 sampling pattern — they are not measured data. [See methodology](#methodology) for derivation.
