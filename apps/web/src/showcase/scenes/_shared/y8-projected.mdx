## Year 8 — Stable ecosystem (projected)

Year 8 is the terminal scene of the eight-year arc. Soil OM projects to **~4.8%**, bird species richness to **~42**, infiltration on the cropped half to **~65 mm/hr** — roughly six times the Y0 baseline. Pollinator visitation in the orchard polyculture climbs to **~35 visits per minute per transect**.

The polyculture is stable. The MDPI protocol's Y9 sampling round will confirm or correct the projection. [See methodology](#methodology).
