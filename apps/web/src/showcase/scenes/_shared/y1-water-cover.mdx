## Year 1 — Slow it, spread it, sink it

Year 1 was water and cover. Three keyline swales were cut along the contours of the three creek catchments. The first winter rye + crimson clover mix went into the cropped half. The hedgerow whips — hawthorn, dogwood, elderberry, serviceberry — were started in the nursery.

By the end of Year 1, infiltration adjacent to the new swales measured **20.6 mm/hr** (up from 11.8). Worm counts on the swale-adjacent zones reached **3.8 per square metre**. Bird species observed climbed to 13 — three new arrivals included a kingfisher working the now-pooling water.
