# Three Streams Farm

A fictional ~180-acre Ontario farm on the Sixteen Mile Creek watershed, rehabilitated over eight years from degraded continuous-corn ground into a working polyculture. Built inside OLOS — the same platform you can use to plan, design, and steward your own land.

Three tributaries cross the lot. Each one is being given back its room to slow, spread, and sink water — the foundation of every other regeneration this farm undertakes.
