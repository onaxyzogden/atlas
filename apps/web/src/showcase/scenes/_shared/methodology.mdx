## Methodology

<a id="methodology"></a>

Three Streams Farm is a **fictional** Ontario operation. Its real-data substrate (parcel polygon, soil maps, watershed boundary, climate normals, AAFC crop history) is pulled live from Conservation Halton, OMAFRA CanSIS, the Ontario Hydro Network, AAFC, NRCan, and ECCC. Its measured Y0 → Y2 monitoring readings are seeded into the OLOS regeneration-events store and follow the **MDPI Apricot Lane Y0/5/9 protocol** end-to-end.

Y5 and Y8 trajectory anchors are **forward-projected** — they are not measured data. They are derived from the canon milestones in [the Three Streams Farm canon doc](/wiki/entities/three-streams-farm) and the MDPI Apricot Lane Farms Y5 and Y9 sampling-round results. When the real Y5 (2029) and Y9 (2033) sampling rounds happen in the demo simulation, the projection will be replaced with the measured readings.

Nothing on this page should be read as a return projection, financial forecast, or investment thesis. It is a stewardship narrative.
