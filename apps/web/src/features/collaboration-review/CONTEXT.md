## Purpose
Collaboration, teamwork, and review surfaces for Atlas projects. Covers
role-based access, map and feature-level commenting, suggest-edit /
markup tools, task assignment and approval workflows, version comparison
and change log, internal vs public views, team activity feed and sign-off
by stage, exporting comments with reports, view-only shareable links,
simplified stakeholder / community review, and meeting presentation mode.
"Design gets messy with more than one human. Includes offline/low-tech
support."
