## Purpose
Deliver the outward-facing, narrative-driven public experience for a project
— a branded landing page, guided tour, story overlays, donation and
interest-capture CTAs, and curated data views safe for general audiences.
This is the "front of house" surface that turns an Atlas project into a
shareable story for supporters, investors, landowners, volunteers, and the
wider public. Distinct from the authenticated project workspace.

## Known gotchas
- **Legacy `features/portal/` + `routes/portal/` are NOT this section.**
  Those are Section 1's project portal / public shareable surfaces
  (authenticated-share links, token-gated read-only views). Section 27 is
  the *storytelling* portal — narrative, branded, MT-flavored — and lives
  under the `public-portal` slug to keep the two concerns separate.
  Do not merge routes, stores, or components across the two.
- 5 of 10 features are `MT`-gated. The UI must no-op / hide MT features
  when `ATLAS_MOONTRANCE` is off; the API route gate (`requirePhase('MT')`)
  will 404 the corresponding endpoints. Do not leak MT copy into the P4
  surface.
- Public-safe data masking (`public-safe-data-masking`) interacts with
  every data-bearing feature in this section — curated hotspots only, no
  raw scoring or parcel-attribute dumps. Treat it as a cross-cutting
  concern, not a standalone screen.
