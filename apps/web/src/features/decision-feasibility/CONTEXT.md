## Purpose
The layer that prevents fantasy buildouts from eating real money. Surfaces
feasibility summaries, constraint checklists, missing-information
checklists, domain-specific feasibility notes (access / water / ag /
livestock / hospitality / education / energy / terrain), and aggregate
decision scores (capital intensity, operational complexity, seasonal
vulnerability, phasing realism, maintenance complexity). Anchors the
"good fit / poor fit", "best use", "not recommended uses", "what must
be solved first", and "what can wait" narrative outputs that sit on top
of the Site Assessment and Scoring layers.
